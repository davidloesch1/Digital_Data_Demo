What you know

Facts, answers, and confidence

You’ll read five statements. For each, choose whether you think it is True or False (keys T and F work while choosing), then say how sure you are that you answered correctly (~50% / ~70% / ~90%).

The lab uses your answers, confidence, and response times to describe calibration—roughly, how well your “I’m sure” lines up with when you’re actually right. This is not a test of how smart you are, and there is no reward for “faking” high confidence.
