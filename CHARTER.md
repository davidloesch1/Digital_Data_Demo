# Project North Star: The Behavioral DNA Charter (v2.1)

## 1. Mission: The Discovery Engine
The Digital Data Demo is a gamified showcase for **Behavioral Intelligence**. We move beyond tracking "what" users do to decoding "how" they feel and "who" they are through kinetic DNA.

## 2. Core Pillars
* [cite_start]**Benchmarked Discovery:** We use the "Nexus Four" (Surgeon, Sprinter, Optimizer, Architect) as benchmarks for the public, but the underlying engine is designed to discover *emergent* clusters specific to any dataset [cite: 1, 131-132].
* **Gamified Transparency:** Users are informed and engaged. [cite_start]Challenges (Reading, Comparison, Friction) are designed to elicit authentic, real-world kinetic signatures [cite: 1, 55-61].
* [cite_start]**Infographic Storytelling:** The dashboard must be accessible, visual, and interactive, providing an "Aha!" moment for both laypersons and organizational leaders [cite: 1, 131-132].

## 3. The "Hook"
The experience serves as a portfolio piece to demonstrate how organizations can identify latent intent and friction within their own platforms using our Behavioral DNA technology.
